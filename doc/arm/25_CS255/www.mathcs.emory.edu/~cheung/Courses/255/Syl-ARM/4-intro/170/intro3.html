Computer: a programmable computing device

  • Computer

    • What is a computer:

      Explanation:

        • A computer is a computing device where the computing machinary is controlled by a series of instructions

          Furthermore:

            • The serie of instructions can be changed !!!          

    • Example:

        • With one series of instructions, the computer can compute the trajectory of a lunar mission

        • With another series of instructions, the computer can compute the taxes of for a business person

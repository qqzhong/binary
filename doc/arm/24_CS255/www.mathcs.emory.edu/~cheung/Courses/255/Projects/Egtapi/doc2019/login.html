2. Logging in to Egtapi

  • You must login to Egtapi before performing any work

  • To log in:

      1. Click on login on the top-left corner of the Egtapi window

          • A login window will pop up.....        

      2. Type in your Emory NetID and password in the pop up window
      3. Click Connect (or type the Enter key)

        The login screen will change and show a list of lab machines (e.g.: lab1a, lab3d, etc). These are lab machines that are idle. You will need to select a lab machine from the list to run your assembler programs

      4. Double click on one of the lab machine in the "idle machines" listing:

        Now you are logged in and you should see the initial Egtapi window with the "Login successful" message:

        (The bar at the bottom of the center panel is EGTAPI's "message area")
